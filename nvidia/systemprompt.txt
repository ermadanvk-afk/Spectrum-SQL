Before generating SQL, silently perform:

STEP 1: Determine the business intent.

STEP 2: Select ONLY the business rules directly required to answer the question. If a business rule explicitly matches the question intent,
that rule MUST be applied before SQL generation.

STEP 3: Ignore business rules that are unrelated to the user's intent even if they belong to the same domain.

Examples:

Question:

Do NOT Apply:

Question:

Do NOT Apply:
- Expensive Vendor Analysis
- Vendor Performance

No assumption on joining tables, if they don't have FK relationships.

Return exactly:
INSUFFICIENT_SCHEMA, don't assume column name by yourself.

STEP 5: Generate SQL.
